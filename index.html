Our Story

Creativity Meets Wonder 🌈

At Making Faces NZ, we create unforgettable moments where creativity meets wonder. Founded in Wellington by mum-of-three Tina — whose children became her earliest (and most wiggly!) canvases — this business blossomed from playful beginnings into a vibrant team of artists and entertainers.

In 2017, Tina trained professionally in face painting, sharpening her craft and igniting a vision. Today, Making Faces NZ is Wellington's most loved children's entertainment company, known for turning ordinary events into extraordinary experiences.

Whether we're painting a superhero, twisting a balloon unicorn, or making bubbles dance, our favourite moment is always the big reveal — watching eyes light up in pure delight. 🎨✨

Good to Know

Health, Safety & Policies 📋

🛡️ Health & Safety

We use only professional-grade, hypoallergenic, non-toxic face paints and glitters that are cosmetic-grade and safe for children. All equipment is sanitised between use.

Our artists are professional and experienced in working with children of all ages. We carry insurance and maintain high health and safety standards at every event.

❌ Cancellation Policy

A 30% deposit is non-refundable and required to secure your booking date. Without the deposit, we cannot guarantee your spot.

The balance is due 5 days before your event. Bookings made within 5 days require full payment. Cancellations after payment are subject to our standard terms. Please contact us if you need to reschedule.

📍 Service Areas

We are based in Tawa and Porirua, Wellington, and travel is included for bookings in these areas. We happily travel further across the Wellington region.

Travel charges apply outside our base area at approximately $1 per km. All Party Combo packages include travel at no extra charge! Contact us for a custom quote.

💳 Booking & Payment

Book easily via our online booking form. We accept payment plans for larger bookings, and offer discounts for not-for-profit and fundraising events.

Custom pricing is available for commissioned paintings and special requests. Have an unusual idea? Just ask — we love a creative challenge!
